Crossovers (3 cars)

Coupe (1 car)

A coupé or coupe is a passenger car with a sloping or truncated rear roofline and two or three doors. Coupé was first applied to horse-drawn carriages for two passengers without rear-facing seats. The term coupé comes from the French translation of "cut".

The coupé body style originated from the berline horse-drawn carriage. The coupé version of the berline was introduced in the 18th century as a shortened ("cut") version with no rear-facing seat.
